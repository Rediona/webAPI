About this page


The purpose of this web application is to depict the different stages of hematopoiesis i.e. the processes of erythropoiesis, thrombopoiesis, lymphopoiesis and myelopoiesis. This is achieved by presenting the different cell types which are capable of initiating the development and maturation of each cell line, as well as the finally differentiated cells.
